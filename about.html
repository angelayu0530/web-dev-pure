About In-N-Out Burger

In-N-Out Burger is a regional chain of fast food restaurants with locations primarily in California and the Southwest. Founded in 1948, it is known for its simple menu, fresh ingredients, and commitment to quality. It is also extremely cheap compared to different fast food chains. It is best known for it's double double.
